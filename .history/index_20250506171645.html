ASCII ART CONVERTER


ASCII art is a picture made entirely out of text characters instead of pixels. This program scans an image and looks at the brightness and color of each small block of pixels. Then it chooses a letter, symbol, or punctuation mark that best matches how dark or light that block is.


The resolution slider controls how detailed the art is.


Lower values mean higher detail: smaller blocks, more characters and the ASCII image looks more like the original.


Higher values mean lower detail: bigger blocks, fewer characters, and a chunkier or more abstract version of the image
